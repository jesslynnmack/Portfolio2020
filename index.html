A Little Bit About Me

Hi! My name is Jessica and I'm a full-stack developer with a passion for development and design. My goal is to always build products that provide pixel-perfect, performant experiences. Shortly after graduating from Georgia Tech Coding Bootcamp, I joined the marketing team at SpecialtyCare where I worked on a wide variety of interesting and meaningful projects on a daily basis.

I freelanced for Motus Creative Group and gained experience working directly with clients and leading project planning and execution.

I currently work at Summit Education where I am the Lead Full-stack Developer. I collaborate with many different teams from marketing to customer service to troubleshoot and problems as they arrive and implement any new ideas and solutions.

When I'm not on the computer, I enjoy being outdoors, live music, and petting dogs.
